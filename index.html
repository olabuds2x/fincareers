The Opportunity

What Is AML?

Anti-Money Laundering (AML) is how banks prevent criminal money from entering the financial system. Every Canadian bank is legally required to have AML compliance teams — and they're actively hiring. You don't need a finance degree: AML analysts come from all backgrounds. With FINTRAC tightening regulations, demand for qualified compliance professionals has never been higher.
