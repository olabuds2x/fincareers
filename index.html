The Opportunity

What Is AML?

Anti-Money Laundering (AML) is how banks and financial institutions prevent criminal money from flowing through the financial system. Every bank in Canada is legally required to have AML compliance teams — and they're hiring.

The best part? You don't need a finance degree to start. AML analysts come from all backgrounds — what matters is training, attention to detail, and understanding how the Canadian regulatory system works.

With FINTRAC (Canada's financial intelligence unit) tightening regulations, the demand for qualified compliance professionals has never been higher.
